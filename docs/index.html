Master Person Index (MPI) Business Profiler¶

Introduction¶

Enterprise Master Persin Index (EMPI) is being used to provide a service to requesting sites for their enrollees. Enrollees are employees of that site that are enrolled in a health insurance plan with a service provider. The input contains serialized partial demographic information of enrollees. This service augments the enrollee information with a Medical Record Number (MRN) so that the site can link the medical information with the enrollee.

Refer MPI-Pilot charter for further information.
